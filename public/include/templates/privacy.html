We collect information from you when you register on our site or enter information on our site. The only information we possess are those you directly give us when you sign up and fill in your profile (optional).

The only way your information is used is to make your experience on our website possible.

All stored password are encrypted. Sadly, the messages you send using our website aren't encrypted at the moment.
